Acacia

Starting from Rs-1500.00 per cubic Meter

Originally a very hard, strong and high density timber, the species in India has been cloned with Eucalyptus and made softer. Thus achieving a balanced timber just between a very hard and a very soft wood, making the timber a moderately hard wood that is well sustained.


RoseWood

Starting from Rs-10000 per cubic Meter

The timber grows extensively in the norther part of Karnataka in the western ghats, one of the strongest hard wood with a very dense heart wood, next only to Ebony.


Yellow Teak

Starting from Rs-2300 per cubic Meter

A yellow and light coloured timbed from the exotic teak family! Refer Dandeli Teak page for Teak characteristic.


Indian-laurel

Starting from Rs-2400 per cubic Meter

A very hard, strong and high density timber. The Indian version of Laurel family of timber. Available throughout India and also in Burma but of varied densities depending on the region of growth.


Sagwan Teak wood

Starting from Rs-8000 per cubic Meter

Sagwan wood is much better than ordinary wood, that's why it is used in costly furniture and interiors. This wood is also called teak wood . The different types of wood in India are legendary for the quality they add to the furniture
